About the Association

More than 15 years of community service, building capacities and empowering individuals

+15 Years

A non-profit civil organization dedicated to serving the community and developing its capabilities, striving to create a positive and sustainable impact through programs and initiatives focused on people, based on values of professionalism, transparency, and collaboration

Vision

An aware and integrated society, in which the association effectively contributes to achieving sustainable development and serving humanity

Mission

Providing quality initiatives and programs that meet community needs, enhancing social responsibility through institutional work and effective partnerships

+5,000 Beneficiaries
+200 Volunteers
+50 Programs
+30 Partners

Strategic Goals

Summary of strategic goals

Institutional Capacity Development

Enhancing staff and volunteer competencies, improving the institutional work environment to achieve quality and excellence in performance

Enhancing Community Impact

Developing financial resources, diversifying income sources, and improving administrative systems to ensure performance efficiency and continuity
