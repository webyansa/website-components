Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is a trust. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data when you use Webyan.

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Webyan, a specialized Saudi digital platform that empowers charities, nonprofit entities and their stores to build a unified corporate presence covering websites, donation portals, online stores and governance — operated by Raneen Development for Business Services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and respecting your personal data, in line with the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and international best practices.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: full name, email, mobile, organization name, entity type, region.
  • Subscription & billing: selected plan, subscription date, payment method, last four digits of the card. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage data: login activity, dashboard pages visited, actions taken, display preferences.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser, OS, device identifiers, browser language.
  • Content you upload: texts, images, logos, reports and governance files published on your site.
  • Communications: messages to support, technical requests, sales inquiries.

3. How we use your data

  • Operating the Webyan platform and providing access to your dashboard.
  • Managing subscriptions, renewals, invoices and receipts.
  • Providing technical support and responding to inquiries.
  • Improving platform performance and developing new features.
  • Sending essential operational notifications (security updates, scheduled downtime, policy changes).
  • Sending optional marketing content and newsletters — you may unsubscribe at any time.
  • Complying with applicable Saudi laws and regulations.
  • Preventing fraud and protecting the platform and its customers.

4. Sharing data with third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share the minimum data necessary with trusted service providers operating on our behalf under confidentiality agreements: payment providers, cloud hosting, analytics tools, government and judicial entities (when legally required), and legal/audit consultants within strict compliance scope.

5. Donations & store data

When donors or customers use a portal operated through Webyan, their data belongs to the subscribing organization (the "data controller"). Webyan acts as a "data processor" on its behalf. Each subscribing organization commits to publishing its own privacy policy. We do not use donor/customer data for our own marketing.

6. Cookies

We use essential, functional, analytical and (optional) marketing cookies. You can control cookies via your browser settings; disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

7. Data security

We apply strict security measures including HTTPS / TLS 1.2+, encrypted password hashing (Argon2 / bcrypt), encrypted daily backups, role-based access control, continuous monitoring, and regular security training for our team. In the event of an incident affecting your data, we will notify you and the relevant authorities within the timeframes set by law.

8. Data retention

  • Active accounts: for the duration of your subscription.
  • Cancelled subscriptions: up to 90 days, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Invoices & financial records: 10 years per Saudi tax regulations.
  • Security & access logs: up to 12 months.

9. Your rights

Under PDPL you have the rights to be informed, access, rectify, erase, port your data, object to direct marketing, and withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights contact hala@webyan.sa — we respond within 30 days.

10. International data transfers

We prioritize storing your data inside Saudi Arabia. When operations require transfers abroad, we comply with SDAIA controls and maintain binding Data Processing Agreements with every provider.

11. Minors' privacy

Webyan is intended for adult organizations and individuals (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

12. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes are communicated via email or a prominent in-platform notice before they take effect.

13. Contact us

For privacy questions, contact hala@webyan.sa or visit our Contact page.
