About Webyan

A platform born from the heart of the sector
to serve its entities with integrity

Webyan is more than a website builder — it's an end-to-end project that aims to raise the digital readiness of the Saudi nonprofit sector through tools designed with a deep understanding of how charities work.

Who we are

Bridging the gap between philanthropy and technology

Webyan was born because our team lived — for years — the complexity of digital transformation in the nonprofit world: websites that never get updated, scattered governance tools, reports that never reach their audience, and operational teams burdened with technical work outside their expertise.

We decided to build a single platform that shortens this journey, brings everything a nonprofit needs into one place, and gives them simple interfaces that any team member can manage with ease.

Today, Webyan serves charities and nonprofit entities across Saudi Arabia, and continues to evolve with the heart of the sector and the mind of a modern technical product.

The problem we solve

Real challenges facing the nonprofit sector

Before building the solution, we listened — for a long time. These are the three challenges we designed Webyan to address head-on.

A faded digital image

Many nonprofits create real impact, yet their digital presence doesn't reflect that impact, costing them supporters and partnership opportunities.

Scattered governance tools

Policy files, financial reports and board minutes are spread across multiple channels with no unified platform to present them transparently.

Technical burden on teams

Small teams spend their time managing websites, integrations and technical updates instead of focusing on the organization's core mission.
